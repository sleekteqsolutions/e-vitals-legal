Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

E-Vitals collects health data that you voluntarily enter, including blood pressure readings, blood sugar levels, SpO2 measurements, and related health metrics. We also collect basic account information such as your name and email address to provide our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your health data is used solely to provide you with tracking, analysis, and insights about your vitals. We use your information to display trends, send reminders, and help you monitor your health over time. We do not sell your personal health information to third parties.

3. Data Storage & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Your health information is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We use secure servers and regularly update our security protocols to ensure your data remains protected.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal health data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for research purposes. You have the option to export and share your own data with healthcare providers.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal data at any time. You can export your health records in various formats. You may also request a complete deletion of your account and associated data.

6. Cookies & Tracking

Our mobile application may use local storage to save your preferences and improve your experience. We use analytics to understand how users interact with our app to make improvements.

7. Children's Privacy

E-Vitals is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or via email. Your continued use of E-Vitals after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at sleekteqsolutions@gmail.com or through the Help & Support section in the app.
